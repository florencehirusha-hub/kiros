About Kairos

Students deserve better guidance at one of the most important turning points in life.

Kairos was designed to bridge the career guidance gap by giving students a structured and engaging way to understand their interests, strengths, and future options before selecting an A/L stream.

  • Designed for students, parents, and school counsellors
  • Built around personalized recommendations
  • Focused on the Sri Lankan education landscape
  • Premium user experience with clear insights

Why it matters

Many students make future decisions based on pressure, trends, or limited awareness. Kairos replaces uncertainty with direction.
